Coconut Margarita

TOASTY! YET CHILL! IT'S NUTS!!!

Ingredients

  • Honey optional, for garnish
  • Toasted coconut optional, for garnish
  • 3 ounces cream of coconut (not coconut milk or coconut cream)
  • 2 ounces tequila, preferably reposado
  • 1 ounce orange liqueur, preferably Cointreau or Grand Marnier
  • 1 ounce freshly squeezed lime juice (about 1 lime)
  • Ice
  • Additional lime slice (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. For a garnished toasted coconut rim, spread the honey on a plate and the toasted coconut on another plate. Dip the serving glass in honey and then swirl in the toasted coconut. Set aside.
  2. Pour cream of coconut, tequila, orange liqueur and lime juice in a cocktail shaker. Add a handful of ice. Put the top on the shaker and shake vigorously for about 15-30 seconds.
  3. Strain the shaken margarita into the garnished glass filled with more ice. Garnish with a lime slice. Enjoy!
