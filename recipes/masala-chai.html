Masala Chai

Description

Masala Chai Recipe from pipingpotcurry.com

5 minutes prep time, 10 minutes cook time. Serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 1 ⅓ cup water
  • ⅔ cup whole milk
  • 1 inch freshly grated ginger (about 3oz)
  • 3 teaspoons tea leaves
  • 2 teaspoons sugar

chai masala spices (can be replaced with ½ tsp prepared chai masala)

  • ½ inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 green cardamom pods
  • 2 black cloves
  • 8 black peppercorns

Steps

  1. heat water in a pot on medium-high heat
  2. while water is heating, crush spices using a mortar and pestle or coffee/spice grinder. add the crushed spices to the water (make sure water is simmering befor adding)
  3. grate the ginger directly into the pot of water. reduce heat to medium and bring the water to a boil
  4. add the tea leaves
  5. add milk and stir it in
  6. bring the tea to a boil. once the tea comes to a boil, turn off the gas and cover with a lid for one minute
  7. strain the tea in a cup and enjoy
